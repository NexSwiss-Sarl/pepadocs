Features at a Glance

Innovative, Customizable, and Efficient

Pepadocs offers a range of features designed to enhance your reporting and client management experience:

  • Customizable Report Formats: Choose from over 10 different field types and structural elements to tailor your reports.
  • Comprehensive Database Management: Integrate your Odoo clients, suppliers, products, and more directly in your reports with seamlessly integration.
  • Geolocation Features: Automatically or manually add GPS locations to your reports and view interventions on a world map.
  • Access Management & Conditions: Set custom access conditions to limit who can view or edit certain reports or fields within a report.
  • Customizable Search and Classification: Use powerful search tools and customizable classifications to organize and access your reports efficiently.
  • PDF, Excel, & Other Export Formats: Export your reports in various formats, customizing the fields to be included as per your requirements.
  • Automated Actions & Notifications: Set up automated emails to clients with reports, create specific actions for each event type, and even call your own APIs.
  • Archiving and Easy Download: Archive or download individual or all reports easily, ensuring that your data is always accessible and manageable.
  • Client Accounts: Create client accounts for real-time access to relevant reports via the web or mobile application.
  • Cloud Synchronization: Keep your reports synchronized and accessible on both the web and mobile applications, available to authorized personnel at any time.
  • Digital Signatures: Allow for on-site or remote signing of reports via the application or web platform.
  • Integration with Photos, Videos, and Files: Attach photos, videos, and specific files to your reports, integrating them seamlessly into your documentation.
  • Offline Mode: Enable your team to complete reports without an internet connection, with the ability to send them once the network is available again.
  • API and NoCode Integration: Connect your reports with your APIs and favorite NoCode solutions, integrating additional tools into your collaborative space.
